Terms of Service

1. What This Service Is

Basic Agent Support is a technical support service provided by Bedrock Advisory Group ("Bedrock") for customers running a pre-configured AI agent system. Our focus is keeping your agent operational. If your agent goes down, we diagnose the issue and work to get it back up and running.

2. Per-Agent Pricing

All plans and pricing are per agent. Each AI agent you operate requires its own subscription or one-time support request. A single plan does not cover multiple agents.

3. What Is Included

  • Diagnosis and resolution of AI agent issues, including agent errors, configuration problems, and unresponsive agents
  • Remote troubleshooting via secure, encrypted connection (Tailscale + SSH)
  • Monitoring guidance and basic system health checks
  • 24/7 availability, with initial response within 24 hours of your support request

4. What Is Not Included

Basic Agent Support is focused specifically on the AI agent layer of your system. The following fall outside this scope:

  • Hardware issues. Physical damage, battery replacement, keyboard problems, or other hardware concerns.
  • WiFi and networking. Internet connectivity problems.
  • Operating system maintenance. OS updates, virus removal, driver issues, and general system administration.
  • Custom development. Building new workflows, writing integrations, or extending your agent beyond its original configuration.
  • Feature requests. New capabilities or enhancements are a separate engagement.
  • General IT support. Broader computer support is outside our scope.

5. Service Availability

Support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours.

6. Activation Period

Subscription plans (annual and monthly) become active 24 hours after payment is confirmed. If you need immediate assistance, the one-time support option is available with no activation delay.

7. Remote Access

To provide support, we access your system remotely using Tailscale and SSH. This is a secure, encrypted connection. You maintain full control over access at all times. A separate Remote Access Waiver, included below, covers the details.

8. Third-Party Services

Your AI agent relies on services provided by other companies, including Anthropic (Claude), Google, OpenAI, and Tailscale. Bedrock is not responsible for their outages or disruptions. We will notify you of known outages and assist with recovery.

9. Pricing and Payment

All pricing is per agent. Annual subscription: $420/year ($35/month). Monthly subscription: $50/month. One-time support: $75/incident. All payments collected in advance.

10. Cancellation

Annual subscriptions receive a prorated refund for unused months. Monthly subscriptions end at the current billing period. One-time requests are non-refundable once work has begun. Contact support@bedrockadvisorygroup.com to cancel.

11. Limitation of Liability

Service is provided "as-is." You are responsible for backups. Bedrock is not liable for data loss, pre-existing conditions, third-party outages, or consequential damages. Total liability is capped at fees paid in the preceding 12 months.

12. Service Modifications

Bedrock will provide at least 30 days written notice before changes to terms, pricing, or scope.
